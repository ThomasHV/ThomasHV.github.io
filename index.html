Problem Statement: Personal Fitness

Our users want to exercise with proper form but are not familiar with the exercises for particular muscle groups. Our solution aims to inform first time gym users with curated exercise routines, pre-workout stretches, and fitness tracking.

Affinity Diagram: Personal Fitness

Our group came together to come up with ideas and categories which could be used to describe our solution.

Persona: Personas for Personal Fitness

Our group thought up examples of typical users who would use our Personal Fitness soultion.

Personal Fitness: Storyboards

Our group created a storyboard for our respective personas to give them a more believable background. This helps us visualize an average user of our app.

Personal Fitness: Sketches

Each individual within the group drew a basic design of how we wanted the app to look. These were then ordered by preference.

Personal Fitness: Prototype

Each individual within the group combined their ideas to create one full prototype of our app. The design ideas from each of our sketches were combined into one.

Personal Fitness: Final Presentation

Our group broke down the app into smaller parts and presented their functionality to explain why a user would use our app.
